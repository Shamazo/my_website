My Work Experience

June 2018 - August 2018

NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory

Summer Undergraduate Research Fellow

I worked in the Planetary and Exoplanetary Atmosphers group (3222) with Dr. Glenn Orton. I conducted research on fine scale waves in Jupiter's atmosphere by analyzing data from the JunoCam instrument, which is the visible spectrum camera on the Juno orbiter. I determined their statistical range as well as properties of specific waves. One of my end products was the largest catalogue of finescale waves in the visible spectrum on Jupiter to date.
